Why VampiTest

Your Blood Sugar on Track. With a Vampire Twist.

VampiTest combines essential blood sugar monitoring with a playful, interactive experience. Our device offers you a seamless, accurate way to measure your insulin levels while taking care of your very own vampire pet. Forget about tedious health routines—VampiTest makes managing your health engaging, rewarding, and fun.

No matter what your blood sugar level is, your vampire knows exactly how to react.

Product Features

You Give Blood,
Vampire Reacts

At VampiTest, we want you to focus on what really matters—your health. That’s why we created a playful, interactive way to test your blood sugar. VampiTest takes care of the rest, from tracking your levels to entertaining you with your vampire companion. No hassle, just a fun and accurate health management experience.

General Information

Product Name VampiTest
Device Type Blood Sugar Measurement Device with Interactive Tamagotchi Feature
Display Type LCD Display with 3 Button Interface (subject to design finalization)
Other
Power Source Rechargeable Battery (USB Charging)
Battery Life Expected up to 3 days per charge (dependent on usage)
Size Compact and portable design (dimensions subject to final design)
Weight Estimated at 150g (device only)
Water Resistance IPX4 rating (splashproof design for safe use in the kitchen or bathroom)
Sunlight Resistance Built to withstand sunlight exposure, ensuring your vampire stays safe even on bright, sunny days!

Measurement Specifications

Measurement Range Expected range from 20 mg/dL - 600 mg/dL
Accuracy Anticipated accuracy ±15 mg/dL (or 5% of reading, whichever is higher)
Test Time Approximately 5 seconds for blood glucose level reading
Other
Blood Sample Size Estimated at 0.5 µL
Test Strips Compatible with standard glucose test strips (final compatibility to be confirmed)
Technology Using an advanced electrochemical sensor for precise measurement (final tech specs to be finalized)

Interactive Features

Vampire Companion A Tamagotchi-style vampire pet that reacts to blood sugar levels
Game Bat-Bat—Help your vampire avoid a baseball bat by jumping into a bat!
Alerts & Reminders Customizable blood sugar testing reminders with playful sounds from the vampire
Other
Resting Mode Represented by a coffin that opens and closes depending on the vampire's health (closed when asleep, open when awake)
Personalized Feedback Planned tips and suggestions for better blood sugar management from your vampire
Hygiene Management Reminders to clean the device after use, and periodic "dead body" clean-up for your vampire's hygiene

Design & Customization

Color Options
Cover Material Durable, soft-touch plastic with anti-slip finish (subject to material testing)
Shape Ergonomic, sleek design (final form factor to be refined)

Connectivity & Integration

Bluetooth Connectivity Expected sync with VampiTest companion app for tracking and data storage
App Compatibility Planned availability for both iOS and Android
Data Export Anticipated ability to export blood sugar data to PDF or CSV format for sharing with healthcare providers
Cloud Storage Planned secure cloud storage for historical blood sugar readings and vampire progress
